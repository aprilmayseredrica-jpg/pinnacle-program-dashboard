Anticipated Questions & Direct Answers

  • Q: Why not just give out merch at the Summit to save on shipping?

    **A:** This could backfire. The survey feedback shows that the Anniversary Incentive is a key motivator. Limiting its fulfillment to an event that not all VAs can attend risks making a significant portion of our VAs feel excluded and devalued. The small cost savings of **$1,200** annually do not justify the potential negative impact on morale and retention.

  • Q: How do we address the HMO feedback?

    **A:** The data indicates that **21% of VAs (154 mentions)** feel our current HMO coverage is insufficient, especially for those in provinces. We should investigate a provider that offers better, more accessible coverage or a flexible benefit that VAs can use for other health-related expenses.
