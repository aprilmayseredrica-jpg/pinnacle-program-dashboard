In-depth Analysis

A deep dive into the open-ended feedback reveals the most impactful areas for improvement.

Top Requested Benefits: A Pareto Analysis

The top 4 requests from our VAs account for over 80% of open-text mentions. Focusing on these priorities will yield the highest return on investment in VA satisfaction.

*Top Requests: 1) More Frequent Bonuses, 2) Improved HMO/Health Benefits, 3) Higher Christmas Bonus, 4) Tenure-Based Salary Increases.

Analysis: The Power of Frequency & Loyalty

✍️ The Pareto chart clearly shows that **More Frequent Bonuses** are the top priority for VAs, with **193 mentions**. This indicates that annual bonuses, while appreciated, are not enough to drive consistent motivation. VAs are looking for more regular recognition for their hard work.

✍️ A significant portion of feedback points to a sense of inequity for our tenured VAs. Many feel their long-term commitment is not rewarded proportionally to the starting salaries of new hires. Addressing this **Tenure-Based Salary Increase** is critical for long-term retention and morale.
