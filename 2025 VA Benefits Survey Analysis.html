VA Benefits Feedback

Insights for 2025: What VAs Are Saying

What's Working: High Impact Benefits

  • Anniversary Incentives: 90% of VAs rate these as a strong motivator for a long-term career. They feel **valued and motivated**.

  • Christmas Bonus: 78.5% of VAs are **excited or very excited** about this new benefit. It's a great start!

  • Feedback Confidence: 75% of VAs believe their feedback is **"always taken seriously"**. This trust is a major asset.

Areas to Improve: What's Missing

  • Christmas Bonus Amount: 14.8% are **neutral** or **not excited**. VAs suggest a higher amount, at least **$100**, to feel more impactful.

  • More Frequent Incentives: **"Attendance bonus"** and **"performance rewards"** were the most requested new benefits. VAs want more frequent recognition.

  • Tenured VA Loyalty: Multiple VAs feel their loyalty isn't adequately rewarded compared to new hires. They want a clearer path for **salary increases**.
