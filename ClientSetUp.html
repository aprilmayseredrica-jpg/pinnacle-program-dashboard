VA-lueLens Insight: Tenure Analysis

This data shows *when* you lose VAs. "Client Set-Up" failures like "Client not ready" (46 days) and "EHR/System issues" (222 days) happen *early*. In contrast, "Resignations" for "Health" (636 days) and "Better Opps" (624 days) represent a critical loss of your most experienced, mature VAs. AI is also targeting mature VAs (540 days).

VA-lueLens Insight: AI Deep-Dive

The AI threat is real, measurable, and accelerating, growing 114% from 2024 to 2025. It targets your most mature VAs (540-day tenure) in "Medical Receptionist" roles. The primary impact (68%) is clients "Reducing VAs," not leaving entirely. This is an opportunity to re-skill and up-sell VAs as "AI-managers," not just "AI-replacements."
